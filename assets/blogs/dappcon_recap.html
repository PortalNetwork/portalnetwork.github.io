DAPPCON Recap, July 19th–20th

A preview at MUMEI & KAIZEN

Last month on July 19th — 20th, Portal Network joined a Developer Conference for Ethereum Dapps & Infrastructure along with numerous Dapps developers and enthusiasts in Berlin. This conference aimed to open discussions and provide a learning opportunity for developers who dedicate themselves on building Ethereum’s infrastructure and applications. We highly appreciate Gnosis for offering this opportunity to showcase our achievements and efforts publicly.

This was the very first time Portal Network announced our two applications, MUMEI and KAIZEN. With several years of experiences in blockchain industry, we have found out that the technology is scattered and there is a huge gap between applications and public users.

So to solve the problem, we have taken two approaches, non-technical and technical, and come up with two different solutions: MUMEI and KAIZEN.

MUMEI

Non-technical users can enjoy decentralized services through MUMEI without knowledge of the technology behind. Its ultimate vision is to let anyone use various decentralized solutions with ease, just like how anyone knows how to use a credit card nowadays.

In a highly centralized society we live, most non-technical users would doubt the value of using blockchain, causing them to experience a high barrier in accessing blockchain solutions. But this doesn’t mean that decentralization is not necessary, especially when centralized servers might experience severe issues such as security and overloading, the decentralized network surely reserves its role in the near future.

Portal Network provides MUMEI, a highly decentralized solution package that any users can freely choose according to their needs.

KAIZEN

On the other hand, KAIZEN will be where developers have easy access to different frameworks and tools provided. Blockchain is a cutting-edge technology and even developers encounter barriers while exploiting it. Through KAIZEN, technical users are allowed to learn, deploy, run and manage blockchain applications. Subsequently, foster a healthy, fast-growing ecosystem.

DAppCon Takeaways

#Why do we need a dWeb?

Actually the answer is quite simple: “To fully enjoy the advantages brought by decentralization, we must thoroughly decentralize.” If this has the potential of becoming one of the future business models, having a dWeb is the easiest way to promote yourself and to participate the coming revolution.

#Multi-language guidelines

Through panel discussions, presentations and workshops, we have discovered that there are many developers who don’t speak English. There are still lots of people who can’t understand blockchains due to the language barrier, hence providing multi-language guidelines to let more people puzzle out blockchain technology and utilize it will be one of our future endeavors.

Portal Network is committed to simplify the blockchain technology, let others apply it in different areas, various industries, and keep ahead in the near future.
