DappCon 2018 –A Developer Conference for Ethereum Dapps & Infrastructure.

Portal Network is glad to announce that we will have one of the workshops in DappCon — Developer Conference for Ethereum Dapps & Infrastructure on19–20th July in Berlin . And Portal Network will be the only team from Asia!!

What is this DappCon about?

DappCon is a nonprofit global developer conference focusing on decentralized applications, tools, and foundational infrastructure on Ethereum. Organized by developers for developers, the conference aims to open critical discussions on the next generation dapps and provide a learning opportunity for developers who strive to build Ethereum’s core infrastructure and applications. In a series of panel discussions and presentations, the conference will facilitate discussions around different governance mechanisms, usability and security of self-sovereign authentication models, next steps in the development of decentralized exchanges, recent developments, as well as the future for different scaling solutions. Additionally, a series of workshops on developer tools will reveal their updates and provide learning opportunities on dapp development and scaling solutions.

What is ENS?

ENS is the abbreviation for “Ethereum Name Service”. By registering this open, extensible decentralized Domain Name Service based on Ethereum blockchain, users can interact with smart contract and buy a unique, customized, human-readable domain name for their crypto wallet address.

What is IPFS?

InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) is a protocol and network designed to create a content-addressable, peer-to-peer method of storing and sharing hypermedia in a distributed file system. IPFS was initially designed by Juan Benet, and is now an open-source project developed with help from the community.

How to build decentralized website?

Please follow the steps below to build your own dWeb hosted on IPFS with a unique Blockchain DNS

1. Go to https://web.portal.network/ , create an account, click “CREATE DWEB” and get started !

2. Select the template you prefer

3. Input your personal public information , such as your photo , name , subdomain , something about yourself , as well as our invitation code “PORTALGO” . This will generate the corresponding data structure on Blockchain . Contact us if the invitation code is malfunctioning

4. Preview

5. Download the “Portal Network extension ” at google store. This will enables your browser to recognize the ENS or any other decentralized domain name.

6. Now you can type in the unique Blockchain DNS on the domain url search bar with Portal Network browser extension and view the dWeb you just built.
